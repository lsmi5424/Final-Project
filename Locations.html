NSW Locations

The RFDS has 4 bases in New South Wales:

  • Broken Hill Base
  • Bankstown Base
  • Dubbo Base
  • Mascot Base

The bases are the home for the RFDS crew and aircrafts however, they can land at almost all hospitals or medical centres with a landing pad.
